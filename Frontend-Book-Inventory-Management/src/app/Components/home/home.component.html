Category: Romance

Romance books are a popular genre of literature that focus on romantic relationships and often explore themes of love, passion, and emotional connections between characters. These books typically revolve around the development and progression of a romantic relationship, featuring elements such as attraction, courtship, and often a happy ending.

Category: Comics

Comic books are a form of sequential art that uses a combination of images and text to tell a story. They are typically presented in a magazine-like format, with panels containing illustrations and dialogue bubbles or captions conveying the narrative and dialogue.
